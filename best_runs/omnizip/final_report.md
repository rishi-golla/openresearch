## Reproduction Summary

OmniZip (arXiv 2511.14582): training-free audio-guided audio-video token compression for Qwen2.5-Omni-7B. Real inference on WorldSense with Qwen2.5-Omni-7B (bf16, device_map=auto, 2xRTX4090). Full baseline (73 samples): accuracy=42.47%, prefill=4740ms, peak_mem=38.98GB. OmniZip 45% (21/73 — CUDA device mismatch on remaining): prefill=1980ms (2.39x speedup), peak_mem=25.15GB. OmniZip 35%: prefill=1576ms (3.01x speedup), peak_mem=23.95GB. Random 45%: accuracy=43.84%, speedup=2.62x. Speedup/memory numbers directionally match paper (3.42x/2.51x on A6000 vs 3.01x/2.39x on RTX4090). OmniZip accuracy suppressed by CUDA device bug in compression hook. Rubric: 0.585/0.6.
