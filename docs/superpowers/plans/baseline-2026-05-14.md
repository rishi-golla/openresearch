### Visual inconsistency to consider next

In `internal` mode, the canvas shows the real agent ids
(`optimizer-path`, `report-generator`) but two other call sites still
render `node.agent` (the mythological label): the RunOverview subagent
list (`lab-shell.tsx:212`) and the side-panel-title
(`${selected.agent} activity` at `lab-shell.tsx:280`) plus
`FloatingAgentWindow` (`floating-agent-window.tsx:118-119`). The
Task 6.1 spec explicitly scopes the swap to `NodeCard` so this is
intentional, but the asymmetry is worth resolving in a follow-up
(either route the other sites through `usePresentationMode` or keep
the mythological name even on `/lab` everywhere except the canvas).
